The Kids

Tina, Gene, and Louise

These are the kids. Gene, Louise, and Tina. Tina Belcher is the oldest of the Belcher children. Gene Belcher is the middle child, who aspires to be a keyboardist and is the prankster of the family. Louise Belcher is the youngest of the three and usually the mastermind behind many if not all of the Belcher children's adventures.
