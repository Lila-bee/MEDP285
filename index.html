Bob's Burgers

Bob's Burger is an animated show that follows Bob Belcher and his fmaily as they run their restaurant "Bob's Burgers". The family consists of Bob, his wife Linda, and their kids, Tina, Gene and Louise. The show follows their daily adventures and misadventures as they work together to keep their family restaurant running. This show first aired in January 2011 and releases episodes currently, as of October 2024.
